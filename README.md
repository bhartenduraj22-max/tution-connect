# Tution-Connect
TuitionConnect is a simple full-stack web application designed to help students discover nearby offline tuition teachers, compare their profiles, and book appointments before enrolling.
The idea for this project came from a common challenge faced by many students. While online learning platforms are widely available, offline coaching continues to offer better interaction, personalized guidance, and stronger conceptual understanding for many learners. However, finding a suitable tuition teacher nearby is often difficult. Students usually depend on recommendations from friends, local advertisements, or word of mouth, making it hard to compare teachers based on subjects, experience, fees, batch timings, or qualifications. At the same time, many experienced teachers have limited opportunities to showcase their coaching classes and reach more students.
